Fettuccine

Description

This fettuccine recipe elevates a classic pasta dish to new heights with a creamy, indulgent sauce. This dish offers a perfect blend of rich, buttery flavors and creamy texture, making it a comforting and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • Pasta:
    Of course, you’ll need fettuccine pasta.
  • Butter:
    This Alfredo sauce starts with two sticks of butter.
  • Cream:
    The rich sauce calls for almost a cup of heavy cream.
  • Seasonings:
    The fettuccine Alfredo is simply seasoned with salt, pepper, and garlic salt.
  • Cheese:
    You’ll need Romano and Parmesan cheeses.

Steps

  1. Start by cooking fettuccine in salted boiling water until al dente, then drain and set aside.

  2. For the sauce, melt butter in a skillet and sauté minced garlic until fragrant.

  3. Add a generous splash of heavy cream and bring to a simmer, allowing the cream to reduce slightly and thicken.

  4. Stir in freshly grated Parmesan cheese until melted and smooth, creating a luscious, velvety sauce.

  5. Season with salt, freshly ground black pepper, and a pinch of nutmeg for extra depth.

  6. Toss the cooked fettuccine in the creamy sauce until well coated. Finish with a sprinkle of chopped fresh parsley and a few extra shavings of Parmesan for a touch of elegance.
