Spaghetti

Description

This spaghetti recipe transforms a simple dish into an extraordinary meal with its rich and savory flavors. Each bite delivers a perfect harmony of tangy tomato, savory meat, and fragrant herbs, making it an irresistible classic.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti:
    An 8-ounce package of spaghetti should make about eight servings.
  • Beef:
    You’ll need a pound of lean ground beef. You can use a mix of ground beef and ground turkey, if you prefer.
  • Spaghetti sauce:
    Use a jar of store-bought spaghetti sauce or make your own marinara sauce at home.
  • Butter:
    Use a stick of sliced butter to prepare the pan and to add richness and flavor to the baked spaghetti.
  • Cottage cheese, cream cheese, and sour cream:
    A mixture of cottage cheese, cream cheese, and sour cream is essential for this decadent, creamy spaghetti sauce.
  • Cheese:
    Shredded Cheddar cheese is the perfect finishing touch for this million dollar spaghetti.

Steps

  1. Start by cooking al dente spaghetti until tender, then drain and set aside.

  2. For the sauce, sauté finely chopped onions and garlic in olive oil until translucent, then add a combination of ground beef and Italian sausage, cooking until browned.

  3. Stir in a hearty mix of crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, and a splash of red wine, letting the sauce simmer to develop depth.

  4. Season with dried oregano, basil, and a pinch of red pepper flakes for a touch of heat.

  5. Toss the cooked spaghetti in the sauce, allowing it to absorb all the robust flavors.

  6. Serve with a sprinkle of freshly grated Parmesan cheese and a garnish of fresh basil.
