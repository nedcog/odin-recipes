Lasagna

Description

This lasagna recipe delivers a mouthwatering experience with each layered bite. The result is a comforting,
delight with a perfect balance of savory meat, creamy cheese, and tender pasta.

Ingredients

  • Meat
    This super meaty lasagna has sweet Italian sausage and lean ground beef.
  • Onion and garlic
    An onion and two cloves of garlic are cooked with the meat to add tons of flavor.
  • Tomato products
    You'll need a can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce, and two cans of tomato paste.
  • Sugar
    Two tablespoons of white sugar add subtle sweetness and enhance the flavor of the sauce.
  • Spices and seasonings
    This lasagna recipe is flavored with fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper.
  • Lasagna noodles
    Use store-bought or homemade lasagna noodles.
  • Cheeses
    Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese make this lasagna extra decadent.
  • Egg
    An egg helps bind the ricotta so it doesn't ooze out of the lasagna when you cut into it.

Steps

  1. Begin by saut√©ing a blend of ground beef and Italian sausage until browned, then simmer with rich tomato sauce, garlic, and fresh basil for a robust, flavorful meat sauce.

  2. In a separate bowl, mix creamy ricotta cheese with egg, parsley, and a generous sprinkle of Parmesan for a luscious cheese layer.

  3. Assemble the lasagna by alternating sheets of al dente pasta with the savory meat sauce and the creamy ricotta mixture, topped with a generous layer of shredded mozzarella.

  4. Bake until bubbly and golden, allowing the flavors to meld beautifully.
